Ramen

Ingredients

  • pork
  • pig bone
  • noodles
  • rope
  • eggs

Steps

  1. water the pig bone for about three minutes, pour out the water, and pour in new water, and boil the pig bone to make the soup
  2. tie the pork into a roll shape
  3. after the soup is ready, boil the eggs for about 5 mins
  4. boil the noodles for about 2 mins
  5. put the boiled noodles into pig bone soup, and put eggs on it too

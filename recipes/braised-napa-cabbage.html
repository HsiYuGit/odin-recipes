Braised Napa Cabbage

Ingredients

  • a head of cabbage
  • oil
  • garlic
  • dried shrimp
  • dried fish
  • dried mushroom

Steps

  1. soak the dried shrimp and dried mushroom
  2. put the oil in the pan and fry the garlic until it turns golden brown
  3. put the cabbage into the pan, and then put in the dried shrimp and dried mushroom with water
  4. close the pan and wait for 20 mins

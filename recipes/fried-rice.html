Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • rice
  • oil
  • soy sauce
  • meat: pork, shrimp, etc.
  • garlic

Steps

  1. heat the oil
  2. put in the garlic, until it turns golden
  3. put in the meat and stir fry
  4. put in the rice
  5. chop the rice to avoid clutters
  6. put in soy sauce from the edge of the pan
  7. done!
